From The D To The World

WeSlay began in 2019 as a basement collective of Detroit artists tired of the industry status quo. What started as late-night cyphers evolved into a movement.

We're not just a music group—we're a creative ecosystem. Producers, vocalists, visual artists, and engineers pushing each other to new heights.

Our mission? To redefine urban creativity while staying true to our roots. No gimmicks, just raw talent and undeniable work ethic.
